GearWise Privacy Policy Last updated: April 3, 2026 Summary: GearWise is designed with your privacy as a priority. Your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or share your personal information. There are no accounts, no cloud sync, no tracking, and no ads. On-Device Storage All your data — including trips, gear, health information, and preferences — is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. Nothing is uploaded to external servers. You have full control over your data at all times. No Accounts Required GearWise does not require you to create an account, log in, or use cloud sync. Your data stays on your device and is never shared with third parties. Location Data GearWise uses GPS location only during active hikes and trail searches to provide you with relevant information and navigation assistance. Location data is processed on your device and is never stored on remote servers or shared with any third party. You can revoke location access at any time through your device's Settings app. Health Data Protection If you choose to enter medical conditions or emergency contacts within GearWise, this information is stored locally on your device and encrypted. It is used solely to customize safety alerts for your outdoor activities. This health-related data is never transmitted, shared, or accessible to anyone other than you. Minimal Network Use GearWise connects to the internet only for the following purposes: Fetching weather forecasts for your planned activities Retrieving trail data and information Loading map tiles for navigation No personal data is included in these network requests. These connections are made solely to provide you with up-to-date environmental and trail information. No Tracking or Advertising GearWise does not use any analytics, tracking tools, or advertising frameworks. We do not collect usage data, behavioral data, or any form of telemetry. There are no ads in the app. Data Collection Summary To be clear about what we do and do not collect: Personal information collected: None Data shared with third parties: None Data used for tracking: None Analytics or telemetry: None Children's Privacy GearWise does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, the app is safe for users of all ages. Changes to This Policy If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated date. Since GearWise does not collect email addresses or contact information, we encourage you to review this page periodically.
