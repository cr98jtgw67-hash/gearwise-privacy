Everything You Need on the Trail

GearWise combines trip planning, live tracking, wildlife identification, safety tools, and more into one powerful hiking companion.

🗺️

Trip Planning

Search and discover trails, plan detailed trips with gear checklists, meal plans, and travel logistics. Know exactly what to pack for every hike.

📍

Live Hike Tracking

Real-time GPS tracking with interactive maps, weather overlays, elevation data, and group coordination. Always know where you are and what's coming.

🌿

Plant & Wildlife ID

Identify plants, animals, insects, and fish using region-filtered databases with reference images from Wikimedia Commons. Know what's around you.

🏥

First Aid Guide

Emergency first aid instructions for snake bites, fractures, hypothermia, allergic reactions, and more. Critical info when you need it most — works offline.

🌤️

Live Weather

Real-time weather overlays with animated heat maps, wind trails, and precipitation tracking. Plan around the weather, not into it.

🌌

Night Sky Guide

Constellation guide showing what's visible tonight based on your location. Star maps, constellation details, and visibility ratings.

🎒

Smart Gear Management

Hierarchical gear organization, pack weight tracking, rental options, and verification checklists. Never forget essential gear again.

👥

Group Tracking

Keep your hiking group connected with real-time position sharing, guide scanning, and member tracking. Hike together, stay together.

📸

Fun Photo Overlays

Add Sasquatch, aliens, fairies, and other fun characters to your trail photos. Live AR filters and post-capture overlays — make memories worth sharing.

🏆

Achievements

Earn badges for milestones, wildlife sightings, and trail completions. Track your hiking journey and celebrate your progress.

🤖

Trail Assistant AI

Ask questions about trails, gear, safety, and more. Your smart hiking companion that knows the backcountry inside and out.

📏

Camera Measure Tools

Estimate the height of trees and cliffs or measure distances across water and canyons using your phone's camera. Built-in trail measurement tools.
